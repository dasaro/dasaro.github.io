About

I am a researcher in Logic (PHIL-02/A) working at the interface of formal epistemology and AI, probabilistic and temporal reasoning, argumentation, and logic programming (ASP/ILASP).

My research focuses on developing formal frameworks for reasoning about uncertainty, knowledge, and actions in dynamic environments.
