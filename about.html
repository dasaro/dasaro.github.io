About Me

I am a researcher in Logic (PHIL-02/A) working at the interface of formal epistemology and AI, probabilistic and temporal reasoning, argumentation, and logic programming (ASP/ILASP).

My work explores how formal logical frameworks can be used to model and reason about uncertainty, knowledge, actions, and their effects in dynamic environments. I am particularly interested in developing explainable AI systems that combine the precision of formal logic with the flexibility of probabilistic reasoning.

Born in Palermo, Italy, I currently work between Lecce and Verona, Italy, contributing to cutting-edge research in ethical and symbiotic AI.
